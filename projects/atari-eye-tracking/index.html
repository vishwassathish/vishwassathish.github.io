Where do humans look when exploring for rewards?

Write-up →

Eye-tracking data from human players across Breakout, Montezuma’s Revenge, and Space Invaders shows how attention shifts in the seconds before a reward event.

  • Gaze anticipates reward locations: players fixate on targets before scoring, not after.
  • In Montezuma’s Revenge (sparse rewards) gaze lingers broadly, suggesting exploratory search.
  • In Breakout and Space Invaders (dense rewards) attention concentrates on the scoring zone 0–2 s before reward.
